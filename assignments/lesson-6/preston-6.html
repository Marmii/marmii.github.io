Color Sky 5K

People came from far and wide to participate in this week's annual Color Sky 5k run. Participants ages 8 and up chose to run the 5k while being blanketed by color chalk. The weather was perfect for this occasion and all proceeds will go to benefit one, Miss Emery Kavenaw, from Franklin County who is battling leukemia. Ms. Karina Andersen said of her experience, "It was a little cold, but it was well worth it to see the community come together in such a way to help someone in need, and it was a blast for the runners as well as the people colorizing us."
