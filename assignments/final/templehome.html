Welcome to our Sanctuary!

The Temple Inn & Suites is a hotel chain headquartered in Bethesda, MD that builds and manages specialized hotel accommodations near temple locations of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints around the world. Our owner-operated company caters to temple workers, patrons, and their families by providing specialized services and accommodations that meet the needs of those patrons. The hotel accommodations and services are available to anyone willing to abide by the terms and policies outlined by Temple Inn & Suites. Explore our website to see all that we offer and see that we meet the many needs of temple patrons who come to serve in the temple or who participate in events such as sealings, weddings, receptions, and youth trips. Spotlight of the Week: Brigham City, Utah Temple

Nestled near the Wasatch Mountains and across from the historic Brigham City Tabernacle is the Brigham City Temple. This temple was announced on October 3, 2009 by President Thomas S. Monson and dedicated on September 23, 2012 by President Boyd K. Packer. It is the 151st temple of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints. This temple, along with many others, boasts a rich heritage.The Brigham City Temple is a smaller temple and therefore gives a more intimate setting for your special occasion. Our facility is within walking distance to the hotel, so there is no need for a transport, although transport is available for those with special needs. While this temple does not offer clothing rentals, our Brigham City location offers temple clothing rentals to those who hold a current temple recommend.
Come enjoy this beautiful temple and its amazing views of the sunset!
