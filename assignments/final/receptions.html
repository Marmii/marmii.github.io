Receptions

Enjoy our in house reception services.

Reception Accommodations

When you choose to have your reception at Temple Inn & Suites, you will have access to our personal reception planner who can help you remember every detail down to the last fork. She will go through our offered services where you can pick and choose exactly what you'd like for your special day. Catering is available as well as the option of employing outside catering services. See below for services and options available.

  • Two reception rooms available for use up to 500 and 1000 people
  • On suite kitchen service areas
  • Catering available(see below)
  • Staff available to serve the reception dinner/refreshments
  • Tables and chairs complete with coverings
  • Reception planning assistance
  • Decoration set up and take down
  • In house DJ available upon request
  • In house photographer available upon request

Catering Options

Below is a list of food options available when you choose to have your reception event catered by Temple Inn & Suites.

  • Catering for up to 1000 people
  • 5 exciting cuisine choices available
  • Dessert only options
  • In house chef available for menu planning
